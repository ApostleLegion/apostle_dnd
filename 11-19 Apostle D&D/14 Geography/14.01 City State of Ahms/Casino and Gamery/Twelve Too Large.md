# Twelve Too Large
[Casino and Gamery](Casino%20and%20Gamery%20Overview.md)

## Rules
The objective of **Twelve Too Large** is to get as close as possible to 22 without going over.

The Dealer stays on a 17 higher.

You can bet on winning, dealer busting, or multi-card bust.

**Maximum Players.** 5
**Minimum Bet.** 1 silver on winning
**Maximum Bet.** 500 silver on winning
**Other Bets.** May bet up to 50 silver on other two bets.

### How to Play
1. Roll 2d12

## Rewards
Winning 1/1
Dealer Bust 1/2
Multi card Bust 1/x, number of die used in the bust
